Sieve of Eratosthenes

Efficiently find all prime numbers up to N.

  1. Start with all numbers marked prime.
  2. Pick next prime P (start 2).
  3. Mark all multiples of P as composite.
  4. Repeat until sqrt(N).

Complexity: O(N log log N)
